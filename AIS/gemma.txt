Good question! Some cheeses are safe during pregnancy, while others should be avoided.

• Hard cheeses like cheddar, parmesan, and gouda.

What should I do before my glucose test?

assistant
The glucose test usually happens between 24 and 28 weeks of pregnancy to check for gestational diabetes. There are two types: one-hour and three-hour.

If you were told to do the **one-hour test**, you usually *don’t* need to fast — but avoid very sugary meals beforehand.

If it’s the **three-hour test**, you’ll probably need to fast overnight.

Since clinics may have different instructions, it's always best to confirm with your healthcare provider.
